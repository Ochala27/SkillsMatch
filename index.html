About SkillsMatch

Living in Abuja, I often struggled to find reliable service providers on short notice. One rainy season, our plumbing failed, and after hours of calling around with no luck, I realized there had to be a better way. Talking to friends and family, I discovered many shared the same frustration. This inspired me to create SkillsMatch, a platform designed to make finding and booking trusted, on-demand services easy and stress-free for busy Nigerians.
